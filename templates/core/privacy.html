Privacy Policy Politica de Confidențialitate

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Cubevo Data Concept ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at cubevodata.com. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your information.

2. Data Controller

Cubevo Data Concept (Registration No: 40378484) is the data controller responsible for the processing of data collected through Google Analytics on our website. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@cubevodata.com.

3. Information We Collect

We only collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, which includes:

  • Technical Data: IP address (anonymized), browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and platform on the devices you use to access our website.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including page views, navigation paths, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookie Data: Information collected through cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy.

Important: We do not collect or store any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details. We do not maintain any user accounts or databases containing personal information.

4. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data solely through:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze anonymous information about how visitors use our website. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our website.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use the anonymous data collected through Google Analytics for the following purposes:

  • To understand how users interact with our website
  • To improve our website's design and content
  • To analyze the effectiveness of our pages and features
  • To optimize our website for better user experience

6. Legal Basis for Processing

We process the anonymous data collected through Google Analytics on the basis of our legitimate interests in improving our website and providing better services to our users.

7. Data Sharing

The only third party with whom we share data is Google, through Google Analytics. Google processes this data on our behalf according to their privacy policy and terms of service. Google may process this data in the United States or other countries.

8. Data Retention

The data collected through Google Analytics is retained according to Google's data retention policies. We have configured our Google Analytics to anonymize IP addresses and to automatically delete user and event data after 26 months.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have certain rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to be informed about how your data is used
  • The right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • The right to restrict processing

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to check this page periodically to ensure that you are happy with any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@cubevodata.com.
